About Me

My name is Sohaila Ahmadi. I have a Ph.D. in Islamic Theology and specialize in feminist interpretation of the Qur'an. I am passionate about studying gender in religious texts and exploring how technology can support inclusive scholarship.

Features of Islamic Feminism

  • Emphasis on equality in human dignity between men and women
  • Gender-based interpretation of the Quran
  • Critique of patriarchal interpretations
  • Preservation of religious values with gender justice

Steps for Research on Women in the Quran

  1. Choose a topic (e.g., Women in Surah An-Nisa)
  2. Collect reliable sources
  3. Analyze related verses and Hadiths
  4. Compare with feminist perspectives
  5. Conclude and give suggestions
